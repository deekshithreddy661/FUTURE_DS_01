

SUPERSTORE SALES & PROFIT ANALYSIS DASHBOARD


PROJECT SUMMARY

This project was developed as part of the Data Science & Analytics Internship (FUTURE_DS_01). It focuses on transforming retail sales data into an interactive business intelligence dashboard that highlights sales performance, profitability, customer trends, and regional comparisons for informed decision-making.

🎯 PROJECT GOALS

Examine retail sales performance across multiple dimensions

Discover customer purchasing patterns

Evaluate product category and sub-category contributions

Compare regional sales and profit distribution

Present insights using interactive and user-friendly visualizations

🛠️ TECHNOLOGIES USED

Power BI

Microsoft Excel

Python (Pandas & NumPy)

GitHub

📋 WORKFLOW

Data Collection

Data Cleaning & Transformation

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Dashboard Design & Development

Insight Extraction and Reporting

💡 MAJOR FINDINGS

Technology products contribute the largest share of overall revenue.

Phone sales outperform all other sub-categories.

The West region consistently records the strongest sales performance.

Consumer-focused products drive a significant portion of business growth.

Monthly sales patterns reveal seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.

📊 FINAL DELIVERABLE

An interactive Retail Sales & Profit Dashboard that enables stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven decisions through clear and impactful visual analytics.
